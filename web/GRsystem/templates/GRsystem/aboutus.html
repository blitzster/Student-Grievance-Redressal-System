Christ University Student Grievance Redrressal System

A student grievance redressal cell is a mechanism for resolving issues between students and their college. It aims to resolve problems reported by students and maintain a positive college environment. The grievance redressal system is a mechanism for receiving and acting on complaints or grievances from stakeholders of private or public institutions. It enables prompt actions on any issue raised by them and to avail services more effectively. Students can state their grievance regarding any academic and non-academic matter within the campus through the online and grievance/ suggestion box.
